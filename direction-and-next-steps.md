# Internal Alignment & Strategic Blueprint

## Brand Positioning Statement
Nebula Ideas is a high-end software engineering and operational AI consultancy. We bridge the gap between technical execution and business strategy for growing enterprises, startups, and product teams across Mexico, LATAM, and the US markets. 

We position ourselves against the "AI Guru" market by anchoring our value proposition in **Systems Thinking, Product Engineering, Software Observability, and Lean Methodologies.**

---

## Operational Model & Engagements

### 1. The Wedge: AI Discovery & Clarity Sprint
* **Format:** Fixed-price, 2-week collaborative sprint.
* **Purpose:** Low friction entry point for clients. Redefines their ambiguous technical requests into concrete system maps.
* **Deliverables:** Workflow Bottleneck Map, AI Feasibility Matrix, Technical Architecture Blueprint.

### 2. The Engine: Implementation Partner
* **Format:** Retainer-based or milestone-driven project execution.
* **Purpose:** Acting as an extension of their engineering leadership to build out the MVP, the web infrastructure, or the Agentic AI pipelines.

### 3. The Guardrail: Observability & Optimization
* **Format:** Ongoing advisory or operational support.
* **Purpose:** Ensuring long-term adoption, monitoring system reliability, token costs, and process optimization.

## Immediate Action Items for Website Launch (Stitch Preparation)

### 1. Visual Aesthetics & Design Guidelines
* **Tone:** Calm confidence, technical mastery, architectural structure.
* **Inspirations:** Ghostty terminal cleanliness, JetBrains Mono typographical rigor, minimalist dark/one-dark palette variants. Clean grids, absolute lack of corporate stock photos or neon-glowing robot graphics.
* **Navigation Flow:** Single-page focused approach transitioning into an onboarding/contact intake funnel.

### 2. Operational Assets to Prepare next
* **Intake Questionnaire:** A brief form hooked to the Calendly link asking:
    * What is the primary operational bottleneck you face today?
    * Are you looking to build a new digital product from scratch or optimize an existing workflow?
    * What is your current core tech stack?
* **Case Studies Framework:** Prepare 2 internal/past experience narratives framed around *Context -> System Problem -> Intervention -> Business Outcome (Metrics)*.

### 3. The Language Strategy
* **Execution:** Launch English-first to capture high-value contracts and international perception, with a native toggle or mirror layout in Spanish to facilitate regional closing in LATAM.
